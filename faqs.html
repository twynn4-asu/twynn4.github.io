    FAQs

    Everything You Need to Know.

    What age groups do you support?
    We support youth athletes from ages 6 to 18 across multiple sports.
    How do I register my child?
    You can register online through our website or visit our office during business hours. Mon-Fri 9a-3p ET
    Are there any fees?
    Yes, registration fees vary by sport and season. Please check our Schedules page for details.
    How does my organization get involved?
    Organizations can partner with HuddleUp by sponsoring events, volunteering, or providing resources. Contact Us for partnership opportunities.
    How can I spotlight a team or player?
    You can submit a spotlight request through our Contact Us page. Include details about the team or player and why they deserve recognition.
